Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/03/2026

MEATZE Aula is an educational platform used to manage courses, students, teachers, educational materials, communications, and related academic activity.

1. Information we collect

We may collect and process the following information:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Account and login information
  • Course enrolment and participation data
  • Files uploaded by teachers or students
  • Basic technical data required for platform security and operation

2. How we use information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide access to the platform and its educational features
  • Manage courses, materials, and student participation
  • Enable file import, storage, and sharing features
  • Improve platform functionality, reliability, and security
  • Communicate with users about account and course activity

3. Google account connection

If a user chooses to connect a Google account, MEATZE may request limited access to Google services only for features explicitly initiated by the user, such as selecting or importing files from Google Drive.

MEATZE does not sell Google user data and does not use Google user data for advertising.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when necessary for providing the service, complying with legal obligations, or protecting the security and integrity of the platform.

5. Data retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and maintain educational records where applicable.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

7. User rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data, subject to legal and operational limitations.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: info@meatzeaula.es
